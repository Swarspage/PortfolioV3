# Fix "Building in Public" label alignment in Youtube.jsx

## Problem
The "Building in Public" label (`<p>` on line 84) sits **outside** the two-column grid, above the entire flex container. On desktop this creates a disconnected, misaligned heading that breaks the compact layout.

## Fix
Move the label **inside** the left column (`leftRef` div), directly above the channel identity row. Reduce its bottom margin to keep things compact (`mb-1 lg:mb-3` instead of `mb-3 lg:mb-6`).

### Changes in `src/Sections/Youtube.jsx`
- Remove the standalone `<p>` (line 84) from above the grid container
- Insert it as the first child of the left column div, before the channel identity row
- Tighten bottom margin for compact spacing
